About

Data with a human translation layer

I'm a Microsoft certified (PL-300) Data Analyst currently working within the ACT Government, with hands-on experience developing and maintaining Power BI dashboards, reports and visualisations, and validating data using SQL to support high data-quality standards.

I'm skilled at interpreting data to identify trends and issues, presenting insights in clear and accessible ways for non-technical audiences, and producing reporting that informs operational and strategic decision-making. I collaborate closely with stakeholders to clarify data requirements, work within Agile delivery, and handle sensitive information with care and discretion.

My background spans technical and customer-facing roles in both Nepal and Australia — from leading farm mechanisation projects to government service delivery — giving me a practical, evidence-based approach to turning data into real-world outcomes.

  • End-to-end analytics — from raw ETL in SQL Server to polished Power BI reporting.
  • Data integrity & automation — building reliable, repeatable pipelines.
  • Stakeholder storytelling — turning analysis into plain-language recommendations.
  • Cross-domain experience — government, agriculture, and customer-facing roles.
